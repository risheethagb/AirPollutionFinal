Overall National Pollution Map

This is a heatmap of the concentration various AQI standard pollutants (SO2, PM25, PM10, Ozone,etc) and their
average values per year by unit nationally, based on the 2020 Annual AQI Concentration by Moniter Data report from the EIA.
Hover above the dot to see the state, city, pollutant standard, measurement, and average value of the measures of that pollutant.
